About Julia Tan

More than a real estate agent - I'm someone who genuinely cares about making your family's transition as smooth and stress-free as possible.

When my own grandmother faced the difficult decision of moving from her beloved home of 40 years, I witnessed firsthand how overwhelming and emotional this process can be. That experience opened my heart to understanding what families go through during these major life transitions.

I became a Senior Real Estate Specialist (SRES) because I believe every senior deserves to move forward with dignity, respect, and genuine support. I take the time to listen to your concerns, understand your unique needs, and work at your pace - never rushing, always patient.

How I Help Families

Downsizing with Dignity

Help you decide what to keep, donate, or sell. I work with trusted estate sale companies and donation services to make the process respectful and efficient.

Senior Living Transitions

From independent living to assisted care - I'll help you find the right community that feels like home and coordinate the entire moving process.

Family Coordination

I work with adult children and family members to ensure everyone is informed and comfortable with decisions throughout the process.

Estate & Probate Sales

Sensitive handling of estate sales with experience in probate court procedures. I make a difficult time easier for families.
